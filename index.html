What is a Digital Product Passport (DPP)?

A verified digital identity for each product that keeps lifecycle data in a single source of truth across manufacturing, ownership, and end-of-life.

  • One-to-one digital identity for each product
  • Structured data record accessible via NFC or QR
  • Verified origin, materials, and attribute data
  • Lifecycle tracking from production to end-of-life
  • Single source of truth for product data
